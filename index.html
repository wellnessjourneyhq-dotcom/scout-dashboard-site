FAQ

Before You Choose

What do I need to use this?

A GameChanger baseball or softball stats export (the .csv you download from GameChanger). Upload it here and preview your team for free.

Can I preview before I pay?

Yes. Upload your export and preview your team leaderboard and top player cards before choosing a product. No sign-up required.

What's the difference between the Season Report and the Team Development Report?

The Season Report is a polished 6-page recap — Scout Board, leaderboards, pitching staff, and awards. The Team Development Report includes all of that (12 pages total) plus a coaching analysis: executive summary, strengths & weaknesses, practice priorities with drills, focus players, lineup & pitching notes, and a 30-day plan.

Is it for baseball and softball?

Both. GameChanger exports the same stat categories for each, so everything works identically — travel, rec, Little League, or school ball.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your stats are used only to generate your preview and files. They are never sold or shared, and there's no account to create.

Is this affiliated with GameChanger?

No. Scout Dashboard is an independent product built to work with GameChanger exports. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to GameChanger.
